Fund Thesis.

The SPVH Value Discovery Fund pursues a disciplined, high-alpha strategy by identifying high-potential SMEs at their pre-IPO stage, entering at discounted P/E multiples, participating in anchor book allocations, and executing structured exits within 12–24 months post-listing. Gains are recycled into the next pipeline, maintaining a rolling portfolio of 6–10 active investments targeting a consistent 10X value-unlocking strategy.
