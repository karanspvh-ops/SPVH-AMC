Investment Framework

The SUJI Framework.

The SPVH Value Discovery Fund pursues a disciplined, high-alpha strategy by identifying high-potential SMEs at their pre-IPO stage, entering at discounted P/E multiples, participating in anchor book allocations, and executing structured exits within 12–24 months post-listing. Gains are recycled into the next pipeline, maintaining a rolling portfolio of 6–10 active investments targeting a consistent 10X value-unlocking strategy. Every investment must pass all four pillars of the SUJI framework before capital is deployed.

SUJI
Framework
S
Scalability & Sector Tailwinds
We target high-growth sectors with strong macroeconomic support — manufacturing, energy, infra, fintech, and export-led industries — with inherently scalable business models capable of 3–5x revenue growth over 4 years.
U
Underlying Financial Strength
High ROE/ROCE (typically >20%), strong and consistent cash-flow generation, low debt-to-equity (<0.5x), historically expanding margins, and clean books. No turnaround bets — only proven performers entering their next growth phase.
J
Judicious Valuation
Entry at deep discounts to listed peers — typically at 30–60% discount to sector P/E multiples — ensuring significant valuation arbitrage upon listing. We run full DCF models with conservative assumptions to validate every entry price.
I
IPO Readiness & Exit Visibility
Robust corporate governance, clean promoter background with no legal encumbrances, DRHP-ready financials, designated banking and legal conduits in place, and a confirmed SEBI filing timeline before any capital is committed.

Risk Management

Protecting Capital. At Every Stage.

Pre-IPO investing carries inherent risks. Our risk management framework is designed to identify, quantify, and mitigate these risks at both the deal and portfolio levels — before capital is ever deployed.

Promoter Background Verification
Every promoter undergoes CIBIL, litigation, MCA registry, and media checks. A dedicated legal team conducts reference checks with banks, creditors, and customers before the IC meets.
Independent Financial Audit
We commission independent forensic-level financial reviews of target companies prior to investment — validating revenue, EBITDA, receivables, and contingent liabilities separately from statutory auditors.
Contractual Downside Protection
All pre-IPO investments are backed by shareholder agreements including anti-dilution rights, drag-along provisions, put options (in select cases), and penalty triggers for IPO timeline breaches.
Concentration & Position Limits
No single pre-IPO position exceeds 15% of fund corpus. Sector concentration capped at 30%. Minimum 6 active investments maintained. Listed SME bucket provides intra-fund liquidity buffer.
Board-Level Portfolio Monitoring
SPVH nominates a board observer in every investee company. Monthly operational updates, quarterly financial reviews, and real-time monitoring of IPO timeline milestones by the investment team.
Structured Exit Planning
Every investment has a pre-defined exit waterfall — primary exit (IPO), secondary exit (strategic sale or block deal), and contingency exit (put option or secondary market) — ensuring capital return in all scenarios.
