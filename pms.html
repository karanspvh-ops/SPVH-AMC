What is Portfolio Management Services?

Portfolio Management Services (PMS) is a professional investment service where a SEBI-registered portfolio manager manages your equity portfolio on a discretionary or non-discretionary basis — tailored to your specific financial goals, risk appetite, and investment horizon.

At SPVH AMC, our PMS offering is built on deep fundamental research, concentrated high-conviction positions, and an absolute return mindset. We manage your portfolio like it is our own capital.

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is the minimum investment for SPVH PMS?

As per SEBI regulations, the minimum investment amount for entering a PMS agreement is ₹50 Lakhs. This can be brought in as cash or a pre-existing portfolio of approved securities.

How is PMS different from Mutual Funds?

Unlike mutual funds which pool money, a PMS offers personalized asset management. You own the stocks directly in your demat account, providing total transparency, possible customization, and precise tax-efficiency tailored to you.

Can I customize my PMS portfolio?

Yes, within reasonable limits based on our active strategy restrictions, portfolios can be customized to exclude certain sectors or hold onto legacy stocks considering your unique tax positions.

How do I track my portfolio performance?

Clients receive detailed monthly performance reports via email. You also gain 24/7 access to our online investor portal featuring real-time NAV tracking, transaction history, and attribution summaries.

What are the fees for SPVH PMS?

Our fee structures are transparent and mutually aligned. We offer fixed-fee and performance-linked models. The detailed fee schedule is provided in our PMS disclosure document during onboarding.

Is my investment safe with SPVH AMC?

SPVH AMC operates as a strict fiduciary. Stocks are held in a demat account under your name by an independent SEBI registered custodian, completely segregating your wealth from our company accounts.
