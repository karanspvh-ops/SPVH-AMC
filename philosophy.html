HOW WE THINK

Conviction Over Consensus.
Patience Over Noise.

We Invest Like It's Our Own Capital.

At SPVH AMC, our investment philosophy is not a marketing document — it is a living framework that governs every rupee we manage. It has been shaped by decades of market experience, multiple economic cycles, and an unwavering commitment to our investors' long-term interests.

We believe that sustainable wealth creation requires three things above all else — intellectual honesty, research depth, and the courage to be different when the data supports it.

Our philosophy is not trend-chasing. It is not benchmark-hugging. It is a disciplined, repeatable process designed to compound capital across market cycles.

“

The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient.

— Warren Buffett

Our philosophy in three words: Research. Conviction. Patience.

OUR PILLARS

Four Pillars of Our Investment Framework.

01

Fundamental Research

We build our own models. We visit companies. We talk to managements, competitors, and suppliers. Our research is proprietary and deep.

  • Bottom-up stock analysis
  • Scuttlebutt research methodology
02

Portfolio Construction

We build concentrated, high-conviction portfolios. Typically 15–25 positions in equity strategies. Each position sized by conviction and risk-adjusted return potential.

  • Risk-adjusted position sizing
  • Sector & macro overlay
03

Risk Management

Capital preservation is paramount. We define risk as permanent loss of capital — not short-term volatility. Every portfolio has hard risk limits built in.

  • Drawdown limits per strategy
  • Continuous portfolio stress testing
04

Exit Discipline

Knowing when to sell is as important as knowing what to buy. We exit when the thesis breaks, valuation becomes unreasonable, or better opportunities emerge.

  • Thesis-driven exit triggers
  • Valuation discipline at entry and exit
