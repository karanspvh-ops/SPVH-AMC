TAX & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

Tax-Efficient. Fully Compliant.

SPVH's offshore platform is built around India's regulatory framework — every rupee invested offshore is legally structured and optimally taxed.

LRS Route — USD 250,000 per Year

Under RBI's Liberalised Remittance Scheme, Indian residents can remit up to USD 250,000 per financial year for overseas fund investments. A family of four can deploy USD 1M annually.

Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements

India has DTAAs with 90+ countries. Our structures are designed to leverage treaty benefits — avoiding double taxation on dividends, capital gains, and interest income at source.

Schedule FA Reporting (ITR)

Indian residents holding offshore assets must disclose in Schedule FA of their ITR. Our team provides annual Schedule FA data packages to ensure full income-tax compliance.

ODI Route for Corporates & Trusts

Indian companies and trusts can invest through the Overseas Direct Investment route under FEMA — with higher limits and specific structural requirements handled completely by our team.

Important Regulatory Information

All offshore investments facilitated by SPVH Asset Management are subject to compliance with the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA) 1999, RBI Master Directions on Liberalised Remittance Scheme, and applicable SEBI regulations for funds domiciled in GIFT IFSC.

Investors are required to ensure personal tax compliance including Schedule FA disclosure, FATCA self-certification, and Common Reporting Standard (CRS) declarations as applicable to their residency status.

SPVH strongly recommends engaging independent tax advisors for personalised advice on offshore investment taxation. This information is educational only and does not constitute tax or legal advice.
